WHY EDUCATE KIDS?

If we want to see the world as a just and fair place where everyone is given equal opportunities, education is what we require. Education is a must if we want to do away with the existing differences between different social classes and genders. It opens a whole world of opportunities for the poor so that they may have an equal shot at well paying jobs, And if we must be successful education must begin from a very young age.

OUR MISSION

To contribute to social harmony and peace by empowering less privileged children with quality education
